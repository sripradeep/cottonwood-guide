# Cottonwood AZ Guest Guidebook — Design Brainstorm

## Three Stylistic Approaches

### 1. Desert Terroir
A warm, earthy aesthetic drawing from Arizona's high desert palette — terracotta, sage, sandstone, and deep wine-red. Inspired by the wine country and ancient Sinagua culture. Feels like a hand-crafted travel journal.
**Probability:** 0.07

### 2. Modern Southwest Lodge
Clean, editorial layout with bold typography, generous whitespace, and rich photography. Inspired by boutique travel magazines and upscale lodge branding. Feels aspirational and curated.
**Probability:** 0.04

### 3. Vintage Postcard Revival
Retro-inspired with a warm cream background, serif typography, illustrated map elements, and vintage badge motifs. Feels nostalgic and charming — like a handwritten letter from a beloved destination.
**Probability:** 0.02

---

## Chosen Approach: Desert Terroir

**Design Movement:** Organic Southwest Modernism — earthy textures, warm tones, and artisan craft sensibility

**Core Principles:**
1. Warmth over sterility — every element should feel sun-warmed and tactile
2. Generous, unhurried layouts that invite guests to linger and explore
3. Photography-first design — images carry the emotional weight
4. Hierarchy through contrast — bold display type against quiet backgrounds

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: Deep Terracotta `oklch(0.55 0.14 38)` — the color of Arizona red rock at dusk
- Background: Warm Cream `oklch(0.97 0.015 80)` — aged parchment, not clinical white
- Accent: Sage Green `oklch(0.62 0.08 145)` — desert flora, Verde Valley vines
- Deep: Charcoal Brown `oklch(0.22 0.02 60)` — rich, readable, grounded
- Highlight: Dusty Gold `oklch(0.78 0.12 75)` — sunset warmth

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed hero with dramatic photography and overlaid text
- Asymmetric content sections alternating image-left / text-right
- Card grid for attractions with hover reveals
- Sticky side navigation for section jumping on desktop
- Horizontal scroll carousels for photo galleries

**Signature Elements:**
1. Terracotta section dividers with subtle wave/mesa silhouette shapes
2. Circular photo badges with hand-drawn border style
3. Wine-red accent lines and decorative separators

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll with section anchors
- Cards lift gently on hover with soft shadow
- Images reveal captions on hover
- Navigation highlights active section as user scrolls

**Animation:**
- Fade-up entrance for section headings (200ms, ease-out)
- Cards scale from 0.97 to 1.0 on hover (150ms)
- Hero parallax subtle scroll effect
- Staggered card reveals (50ms delay per card)

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif) — for section headings and hero text
- Body: Lato (sans-serif) — for readable body copy
- Accent: Playfair Display Italic — for pull quotes and highlights
- Scale: 14/16/20/28/40/56px

**Brand Essence:**
Your Verde Valley basecamp — for travelers who want wine, wilderness, and wonder within walking distance. *Unhurried. Authentic. Alive.*

**Brand Voice:**
Headlines feel like a knowledgeable local friend whispering insider tips. CTAs are invitations, not commands.
- "Step outside — Sedona is 20 minutes away and the canyon is calling."
- "Pour yourself a glass. You're in Arizona wine country now."

**Wordmark & Logo:**
A stylized cottonwood leaf silhouette in terracotta, paired with a thin horizontal rule. No generic font wordmark.

**Signature Brand Color:** Terracotta — `oklch(0.55 0.14 38)`
